Our Population

SailFuture serves children who eligible for Level I specialized therapeutic foster care. Typically these are children with a history of abuse or neglect, or delinquent behavior, and who have an emotional disturbance or serious emotional disturbance.

Foster Parent Eligibility

SailFuture is always seeking loving and compassionate adults who are interested in making a difference in a young person's life.

Be 21 years of age or older
Own or lease/rent a 2 bedroom 2 bath home (or larger)
Complete 24-hours of pre-service training
Complete 33-hours of specialized therapeutic care training
Pass a home study
Pass Level II background screens
