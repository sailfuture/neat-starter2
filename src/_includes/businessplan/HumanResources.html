Professional Development

The year-round calendar may present challenges to faculty and staff as it relates to participating in professional development offerings taking place during a traditional school summer. Therefore, staff are able to apply for approval to attend a professional development workshop/conference/etc. Approval is contingent upon fund availability, school programming, and term cycle.

A professional development repository will be maintained and regularly updated to provide faculty and staff with access to resources, articles, journals, in-person/virtual opportunities.

Additionally all faculty and staff can submit content to the professional development repository to further the PLC.

All professional development and annual planning, after the first school year, will be data driven; we will use individual and school-wide data, staff observations, narrative feedback, and anecdotal artifacts to drive the focus of programmatic and individual professional development opportunities.
