Our staffing model supports a 1:12 teacher to student ratio and creates a robust educational experience for students through our faculty roster, which includes a STEM, ELA, Construction, Maritime, and Culinary instructors. Students also receive support from an onsite mental health counselor, intervention specialists and the Head of School and director of Social Enterprises.

Staff Onboarding Plan

The Head of School and Director of Social Enterprises were onboarded in February of 2021 and the remaining staff will be onboarded the 2nd week of July to begin (3) weeks of training prior to school opening.

Faculty onboarding in year 1 will focus on conveying the desired culture of SailFuture Academy, provide ample training to understand curriculum and lessons, and clearly explain and effectively prepare for implementing systems of accountability and assessment.
