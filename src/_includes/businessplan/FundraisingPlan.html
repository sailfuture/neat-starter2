Fundraising Plan

SailFuture’s financial model ensures the Academy’s long-term sustainability through a diverse source of revenues, including:

Earned Income through
Social Enterprises

Construction Unit

The Construction SEU has a signed $900,000 contract to complete the renovation of the 2nd floor of the school building and the kitchen and restaurant. The Construction SEU projects $273,050 in net income for year 1.

Future revenues for the Construction Unit will be a combination of supporting SailFuture’s growth (i.e. the construction of independent living homes) and contracts sourced through the business council.

Maritime Unit

The Maritime SEU will operate a 105’ motor yacht for charter in Florida in the Bahamas. A projected $125,000 in charter revenues will offset the $200,000 in annual holding costs.

The vessel is currently owned by SailFuture with no debt and is currently valued at $750,000. The Maritime SEU projects a small deficit in the first 3 years prior to selling the vessel for a net profit of $625,000 in Year 4.

Future revenues and projects for the Maritime SEU are built on projected boat donations. The Maritime SEU is expected to solicit 1 major yacht donation per year.

Culinary Unit

During Year 2, the Social Enterprises Director will launch the Culinary unit.

Revenues for the culinary unit will be derived from catering, meal subscription, and events.
