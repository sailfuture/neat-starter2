Section 04

Governance & Management Structure

The school will launch under the direction of the current Board of Directors of SailFuture, Inc. and transition to a separate Board of Directors to oversee the school in the Summer of 2022.

b The current Education Committee will provide direct oversight of the School in advance of the Summer 2022 transition to an independent Board of Directors.

Division of Responsibilities

In order to fulfill the three primary responsibilities of the Board - to ensure the organization is fulfilling its mission, to ensure fiscal responsibility, and to protect it from legal liabilities, the Board will delegate the day-to-day operations of the organization to the Chief Executive Officer and the team hired by and reporting to the CEO.

The Board will hold the CEO accountable through quarterly board meetings and an annual review carried out by the president of the board.

The head of school will be held accountable by the CEO until the SailFuture Academy transitions to an independent board of directors in the Summer of 2022. After the transition, the Head of School will report directly to the newly formed board.

Board Member Responsibilities

The SailFuture academy will have a 9-member Board of Directors when it transitions to independence in the Summer of 2022. In addition to ensuring the Academy fulfills its mission, maintains financial integrity, and is protected from legal liabilities, Board members are responsible for the following:

Introduce 10 new people to the organization per year (campus visit, event attendance, etc)
Meet minimum personal or solicited contribution level: $15,000 per year
Identify and facilitate speaking opportunities at local organizations to raise awareness
Design, plan, and execute an annual fundraising strategy in partnership with CEO
Attend quarterly board meetings in person or by phone
Be an engaged member of the SailFuture community by building personal relationships with students
