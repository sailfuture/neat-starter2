Overview

Case Management Services & Program Overview

In partnership with Eckerd Connects, and Lutheran Services of Florida, SailFuture provides dependency case management services to the highest-risk teens in foster care in Pinellas and Pasco counties.

Our services

Our Case Managers work hand in hand to help our high risk teens stabilize and navigate their way out of a complex foster care system and into a loving and permanent home.

Who we serve

On average our teens have had 10 or more foster care placements, attended 5 or more schools, and are unlikely to reunify with their families.

They have been referred to SailFuture for specialty case management services because existing providers have been unable to meet their unique needs and manage their challenging behaviors.

Our teensâ€™ complex trauma leads to challenging behaviors. Our team focuses on interpreting and decoding those behaviors to understand the underlying message.

Age Range: 13 - 17 years old
