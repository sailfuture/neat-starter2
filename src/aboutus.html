Our History

2013
SailFuture was founded in 2013 as a sailing-based mentoring program to boost the graduation rate for high-risk students at Booker high school in Sarasota.
2014
Week-long expeditions for youth on felony juvenile probation.
2015
SV Defy the Odds donated and sailed 20,000 miles from Turkey to Florida with highest-risk juvenile offenders in the United States.
2016
The Waterfront House opens in St. Petersburg, FL providing 24/7 residential care to teens in foster care.
2017
An accredited private technical school opens for youth living in a residential program.
2018
Begin international sailing expeditions with new itinerary traveling through Mexico, the Bahamas, Dominican Republic, and Puerto Rico
2019
Specialized Case Management division to serve high-risk teens in foster care. Life Skills Center opens to provide daytime support to teens in crisis AHCA license acquired to provide in-house mental health services
2020
Lakewood Estates group home opens, providing 24/7 residential care to teens in foster care. First domestic sailing expedition north up the eastern seaboard to Maine. First domestic sailing expedition along the gulf coast to Galveston, Texas.
2021
SailFuture Academy, a private high school planned for opening in fall 2021. The SailFuture Academy is a private nonprofit vocational high school for low-income families and youth at-risk in St Petersburg, FL.
