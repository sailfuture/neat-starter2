Summer 2022

A Dream Come True: 100' School Ship Donated

For the past 8 years students have lived in small bunks on a 65' long and 12' wide rocket ship of a sailboat.

That all changes with the donation of SV Wisdom - a 100' long and 24' sailing ketch with 8 cabins, 6 heads, a classroom, and an onboard dive school.

Spring 2021

Old Norwood School transformed into SailFuture Academy

Students and graduates helped lead the renovation of 30,000 sq/ft of office and classroom space at the 99 year old home of the new school.

Fall 2022

The Waterfront House Recieved a New Seawall, Backyard & Pool

A major donor stepped up to fund a new seawall at the waterfront house and the Associated Builders and Contractors of the Gulf Coast brought 50 volunteers to renovate the front and backyard with a new retaining wall, concrete pads, and landscaping.

Fall 2021

We Started A High School -- the SailFuture Academy

We welcomed 50 students to a life-changing 4-year journey in August of 2021. Paid internships, hands-on projects, international sailing expeditions, and a world-class entrepreneurship curriculum prepare students for life and career.

Spring 2022 - Winter 2022

Three National Awards for Innovation

In our first year, The SailFuture Academy earned recognition from three of the most prestigious education funders in the United States.

$500,000 YASS STOP Prize for Innovation

The YASS Prize and the Center for Education Reform recognized SailFuture as one of the most innovative and sustainable school sin the country.

Drexel Fund Foundation

SailFuture received support from one of the nation's leading education funders to design and launch an innovative new private school model.

Stand Together Foundation 2022 Cohort

Our leadership team participated in Stand Together's Catalyst program and shared our story at their signature convening in Denver.
Fall 2022

We started two businesses to generate earned income to fund student scholarships -- $1M in Revenue in First Year!

We launched Impact Social Ventures, a social enterprise specialty marine and residential construction firm in January of 2021.

We employed more than 30 students and generated $1M in revenue in our first year. All net income is donated back to SailFuture to fund scholarships for students at the SF Academy.
