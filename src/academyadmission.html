Admission Eligibility

SailFuture Academy is intended to serve low-income families and children who may qualify for need-based scholarships. Students enroll in the 9th grade, with limited consideration given to 10th grade transfer students.

All eligible youth in Pinellas County are eligible to apply to attend the SailFuture Academy.

  • Resides in Pinellas County
  • Entering 9th grade or transferring from 9th grade
  • Demonstrated student interest in vocational / business education
  • Eligible for a Step Up For Students or McKay Scholarship

Cost of Tuition

All families who are eligible for a Step Up For Students and/or McKay Scholarship receive a full tuition scholarship. Their only out-of-pocket expenses are the $250 registration fee and the $250 transportation fee.

Tuition for families who are not eligible for a Step Up For Students and/or McKay Scholarship is determined using a sliding scale based on family income and expenses.
