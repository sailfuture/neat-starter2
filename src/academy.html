Hands-On and Real-World Curriculum Design

SailFuture Academy is an all-boys, year-round school designed to immediately bridge the gap between graduation and a career. We place students in the driver’s seat to solve real world problems from the moment they step foot on campus in order to re-engage and re-inspire students who have become disconnected from their high school experience.

“The SailFuture Academy is a technical high school that prepares students to graduate into business owners and operators.”

Lindsay R., Head of School

Learn Business by Doing Business

Internships within SailFuture Academy’s three Social Enterprises help our students develop critical social-emotional, business, and industry-specific skills that make them marketable to our employment partners. In addition to on-site training, students participate in apprenticeships with local employers to earn field experience and a financial stipend.
