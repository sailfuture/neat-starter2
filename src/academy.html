Learn Business by Doing Business

Internships within SailFuture Academy’s three Social Enterprises help our students develop critical social-emotional, business, and industry-specific skills that make them marketable to our employment partners.

In addition to on-site training, students participate in apprenticeships with local employers to earn field experience and a financial stipend.
