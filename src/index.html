We transform life outcomes for teens and families.

SailFuture is a comprehensive child welfare agency, and private school based in St. Petersburg, Florida.

Our mission drives us to innovate and develop effective programs for high-risk youth, particularly youth in foster care.

SailFuture Wins $500,000 YASS Prize

Prize officials recognized the school for leveraging project-based learning and paid apprenticeships to prepare students in foster care for successful careers by focusing on the maritime business in addition to culinary and construction experiences.

“The Yass Prize recognizes and rewards outstanding education providers and aims to amplify their work through financial support, collaboration, coaching, encouragement and an extensive mentorship network.”
