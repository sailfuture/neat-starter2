Program Overview

The Life Skills Center provides daytime supervision and support to youth in crisis between 6am and 10pm 7 days a week, 363 days per year.

The Life Skills Center provides daytime supervision and support to youth in crisis between 6am and 10pm 7 days a week, 363 days per year.

Youth attending the center participate in daily life skills activities, ranging from kayaking and swimming lessons to GED tutoring and mental health counseling.

Our objective at the Center is to help each child find stability and hope while placement and case management work to identify a long-term home.

Eligiblity Criteria

Eligibility criteria: Youth in foster care
Gender: Male and female
Ages: 13-17
